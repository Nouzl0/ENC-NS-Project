# MNIST Classification

**Architecture and Normalization Study** — Mendel University in Brno, Faculty of Business and Economics

Five neural network architectures are trained across five input normalization strategies on MNIST, yielding 25 controlled runs under identical hyperparameters. The study isolates the contribution of each factor to classification accuracy, convergence, and computational efficiency.
